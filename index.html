What's LPBT Press Club??

Lycée Pilote Bourguiba Press Club is a Club founded by high schoolers in Tunisia. We do media coverages for events and post writings, interviews, articles, caricatures, and more. Click on the button below to like our page, Thank you!

Overview

LPBT Press Club is a club composed of young high-schoolers motivated and interested in writing. We publish articles about a lot of subjects, news, thoughts and writings of our members and ones that you, our public, write as well (mainly shared on our Instagram account, but don't hesitate to send us your writings on our Facebook page as well!), caricatures, videos, and most importantly the articles and pictures (media coverage) of other clubs' events! ♥ Do not hesitate to invite us to cover your event ;)
